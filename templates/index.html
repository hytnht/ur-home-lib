Services

Quick and easy

Help you manage your book collection and track publish status with multiple support features.

Multiple options

Organize by authors, series, reality position or even cover's color! It's all up to you.

Private storage

It's mainly statistical and no community activities. Everything is on your own.

What Can This Website Do?

Book manager

Mass upload using stylesheet and scanner. Save your time from boring typing.

Series follower

Notify publishing day and stocks in commercial sites. Keep track of the next book in series.

Trade logger

Record all your collection's in-and-out activities. Never forget who borrowed your books.

Position saver

Your little librarian that memorize every single position in the bookshelves.
