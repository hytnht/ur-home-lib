About

This is the final project for CS50 Introduction to Computer Science - a free course by Havard University. You can take a look here.

Those works are referenced from CS50 lessons and problem sets, W3schools and CSS-tricks tutorials. And a lot of help from StackOverflow, for sure.

Some libraries and frameworks used: Flask, Bootstrap, FontAwesome, Dayjs, CS50, Werkzeug, Jinja, SQLite..

Languages: Python, Javascript, SQL, HTML, SCSS
